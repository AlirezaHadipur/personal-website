About Me

My name is Seyed Alireza Hadipoor, and I graduated from the University of Guilan with a GPA of 3.62. My academic interests lie in Human-Computer Interaction and Data Science, particularly Natural Language Processing (NLP).

Post-graduation, I have dedicated myself to acquiring analytical skills such as Statistics and Probability, Python programming (including libraries like SciPy, NumPy, and PyTorch), and SQL. I am currently a research assistant in the University of Guilan NLP group, where I focus on new algorithms for text classification.

In addition to my research activities, I am pursuing courses on Coursera to deepen my knowledge in UX research and design principles. I am actively engaged in reading papers and books about user-centered research and design, and I look forward to connecting with professionals in these fields.

I love fitness and running. I usually read books and watch sitcoms in my leisure time. I also love to challenge myself to learn new things.
